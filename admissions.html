Admissions

At Dabs Dynamic International School, we welcome parents and guardians who seek quality education rooted in discipline, integrity, and academic excellence.

Admission Overview

Admission into Dabs Dynamic International School is open to pupils and students who meet the school’s academic and behavioural standards. We operate a Day School system and follow the Nigerian Curriculum, enriched with ICT, moral training, and sports activities.

Classes Offered

Creche & Nursery

Early childhood development in a safe, caring, and stimulating environment.

Primary

A strong academic foundation combined with moral instruction, creativity, and basic ICT skills.

Secondary School

Focused on academic excellence, discipline, leadership development, and preparation for external examinations.

Admission Process

  1. Parents/Guardians are required to visit the school for registration.
  2. Obtain and complete the admission form.
  3. Submit all required documents.
  4. Entrance assessment and interview (where applicable).
  5. Admission offer and enrollment confirmation.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed admission form
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Previous school report (for transfer students)
  • Passport photographs
  • Medical report
