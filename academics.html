Academics at Dabs Dynamic International Schools

We offer a comprehensive educational experience from Creche to Secondary levels, blending academic excellence with moral values, ICT skills, sports, and extra-curricular activities.

Creche & Nursery

Early childhood education focused on basic literacy, numeracy, social skills, and moral training in a safe and stimulating environment.

Primary School

Comprehensive curriculum including English, Mathematics, Science, ICT, Moral Training, and Sports, preparing students for junior examinations and building foundational skills.

Secondary School

Advanced academic programs, external exam preparation, leadership development, ICT integration, moral guidance, and sports for holistic growth.

Extra-Curricular Activities

Sports, cultural activities, and clubs designed to develop social skills, teamwork, creativity, and personal discipline.

Curriculum Highlights

  • ICT Integration in all levels
  • Moral and Ethical Education
  • Sports and Physical Development
  • Problem-solving and Critical Thinking
  • Career Guidance and Leadership Skills
