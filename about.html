About Dabs Dynamic International Schools

Established in 2006, Dabs Dynamic International Schools embodies Knowledge cum Discipline to deliver high-quality education for pupils and students.

Vision Statement

    To deliver quality education to our pupils/students that seek to:

  • Equip our pupils/students to come out with excellent results in all forms of examinations, external or internal.
  • Make our pupils/students disciplined, God-fearing and confident wherever they find themselves.
  • Imbibe in our pupils/students ability to identify and solve problems in their immediate environment and be agent of change.
  • Help our pupils/students chart a great career path in order to be successful individuals in the future.
  • Bring back the quality system of education of olden days, blended with those modern trends in education which are desirous for academic excellence.
  • Developing world class leaders.

Mission Statement

At Dabs-Dynamic Int’l School, we do realize the fall in standards of education across the country, therefore our mission is to recapture the high educational standards of old by bringing together a team of competent, dedicated and disciplined teachers who will employ modern teaching methods in delivering activities-based lessons, putting the individual needs of our pupils/students at the center of each lesson delivered.

Core Values (I.T.A.C.E)

  • Integrity
  • Teamwork
  • Accountability
  • Commitment
  • Effectiveness
